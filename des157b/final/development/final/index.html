What is "For Us"?

"For Us" is a safe haven for Hmong people to discuss and share their stories through four selected topics: food, art, war, and ghosts. Hmong culture is slowly dissipating due to Westernization and age, so this was designed to safekeep information and stories about Hmong people and culture for future generations.

How to Participate

  • Select "Write" or "Gallery"

  • "Write" will take you to four topics: food, art, war, or ghosts. Select a topic, fill in the information, and submit!

  • "Gallery" will take you to to all the submitted works. Select a topic and view all the stories!

Helpful Advice

It is HIGHLY recommended you write your story in a separate software such as Google Docs, Microsoft Word, or Notes to save your progress. Copy and Paste your story into the "Story" input after you have finished.
