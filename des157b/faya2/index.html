Farmers Are Your Ally (FAYA)

FAYA is a non-profit organization dedicated to fighting drought in California through saving water in order to help support our farmers! Farmers are absolutely essential to the Californian economy and without them, the United States would have even more trouble filling tummies with food!

Let's Help

Farmers need support! They are lacking access to water due to the drought and water shortages. By practicing water sustainability, we can help them prioritize on agriculture and livestock!

Let's save water! California is seriously lacking water due to drought, speeding evaporation, and reckless citizens! Managing your water usage for personal uses, gardening, and cleaning can help reduce water waste!

Drought needs to be stopped! It is a natural occurrence but human hands can join to slow down its effects! By practicing water sustainability, you can slow down the consequences of drought.

Let's Help

  • Take 5 minute showers instead of 10 minute showers
  • When shaving, plug the sink instead of running the water
  • Water plants in the evening to lessen evaporation and preserve moisture
  • Avoid letting lawn sprinklers excessively run long.
  • Use mulch on your plants to reduce evaporation.
  • Harvest rainwater and fog.
  • Plant more trees to promote photosynthesis and improve air quality.
  • Purchase from farmers markets to help local farmers - their produce is fresher anyway.
