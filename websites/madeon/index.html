MADEON

Hugo Leclerq, also known as, Madeon, is a French electronic-dance musician who is now based in Los Angeles. He has produced two full length albums, Adventure and Good Faith, the latter of which was nominated for a Grammy Award. Leclerq is widely known for his song, Shelter, which he created with longtime friend, Porter Robinson. He has worked alongside notable artists, namely Lady Gaga, Mark Foster of Foster the People, and Dan Smith of Bastille.

I have seen Madeon three times during his Good Faith Tour 2019, Second Sky 2019, and Second Sky 2021.
