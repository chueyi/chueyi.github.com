Squid Game Mad Libs

Let's play a Mad Libs story generator that is themed after the popular Netflix show, "Squid Game"! You can select your own character which changes the script! It also features two bonus characters, Jungkook and V, from the renowned South-Korean boyband, BTS.

For Us

"For Us" is a haven for my people, the Hmong, to write their stories in four topics: food, art, war, and ghosts. These subjects are prominent in Hmong culture and should be shared with each other! The inspiration behind this was to preserve knowledge of the Hmong people because the population is small and losing its rich culture to contemporary Americanization.
