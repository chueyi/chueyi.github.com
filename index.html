Squid Game Mad Libs

Let's play a Mad Libs story generator that is themed after the popular Netflix show, "Squid Game" You can select your own character which changes the script! It also features two bonus characters, Jungkook and V, from the renowned South-Korean boyband, BTS.

For Us

This is a haven for Hmong people to write their stories in four topics: food, art, war, and ghosts. These subjects are prominent in Hmong culture and should be shared and celebrated with each other!

Farmers Are Your Ally

Farmers Are Your Ally (FAYA) is a graphic design campaign made to teach and raise awareness about Californian drought and how it can negatively impact farmers then ultimately, our stomachs! This website is a supplement to the graphic design campaign which you can learn more about in the "Graphic Design" section.

A Walk in the Woods

This is an illustrative storytelling project inspired by anxiety from night-time walks after class. A Walk in the Woods showcases the fullPage and Vanilla Tilt Javascript Libraries.
